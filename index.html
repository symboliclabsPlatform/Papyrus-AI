Featured solution

Papyrus AI · Document Intelligence & RAG Automation.

Papyrus turns unstructured document archives into live, queryable knowledge — with pipelines that ingest PDFs, images and scans, extract entities and relationships, and feed domain-specific RAG systems.

How Papyrus works with Google Cloud

Papyrus uses Google Cloud as its backbone: documents land in Cloud Storage, are processed with Cloud Run and Cloud Functions, enriched via Document AI, and stored in structured form for analytics and search. Vertex AI provides embeddings and generative models that power RAG pipelines and semantic search.

  • Universal ingestion of PDFs, scans and images, independent of quality.
  • Structured data extraction for entities, keys and relationships.
  • RAG-ready embeddings for downstream question answering and agents.
  • Scalable architecture that grows from single files to millions of documents.

Built-in security & compliance

As a provider we understand the specific requirements of the German and European markets. All infrastructure can be operated fully in German regions with strict data protection and audit-ready logging.

  • GDPR-compliant data handling with regional storage and encryption in transit and at rest.
  • Fine-grained access control with IAM, service perimeters and dedicated project separation.
  • Backup, disaster recovery and observability built into the platform by design.
Case study · Insurance

DEMV Systems – Automating insurance document flows.

For DEMV Systems we engineered an AI-powered pipeline that automatically extracts insurance data from thousands of PDF documents every day. Papyrus normalises heterogeneous inputs, extracts key fields and exposes them to downstream systems via APIs and search.

The result: dramatically reduced manual processing, higher data quality and the ability to query complex document histories in seconds instead of hours.
